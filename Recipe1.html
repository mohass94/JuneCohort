Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Add in the vanilla extract and eggs, one at a time, until well mixed.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes.
  10. Serve and enjoy the BUSSIN cookies!
